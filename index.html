▶ How to Play ◀

Movement

Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move around the Academy. On mobile, use the on-screen D-pad.

Interaction

Walk up to an NPC or terminal and press E or Space to interact. On mobile, tap the canvas.

Face-Offs

NPCs challenge you to pop culture trivia. Answer correctly to gain REP. Answer wrong and you lose REP. A timer counts down — answer fast for bonus points.

Streaks

3 correct in a row: ON FIRE bonus. 5 in a row: UNSTOPPABLE bonus. Wrong answers reset your streak.

Winning and Losing

Reach your REP goal (default 200) to win. Hit 0 REP and it is game over. Your REP carries across the whole run.

Perks

After each run, you earn permanent REP to spend on perks in the Perk Shop. Perks give you advantages in future runs.
